V60 dipper

The Hario V60 is one of the most popular pour-over brewing methods you’ll come across in the barista world, and it’s the method you’re most likely to see in your favorite cafe. For the novice, V60 brewing is easy to learn, but on the other hand, it has a whole range of brewing variations that are guaranteed to keep any coffee geek with a love of tweaking at the brew level entertained. For example, by playing around with the Hario V60 grind size, you can speed up or slow down the rate at which your coffee is going to be brewed. A finer coffee grind will take longer to brew versus a larger grind that will allow you to speed up your brew time. If you opt for a faster V60 brew time, you’ll end up with a brighter, lighter-bodied cup.
